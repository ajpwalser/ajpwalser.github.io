Info

Adam lives in a very windy city in southern Alberta, Canada with his partner and daughter who are seemingly convinced it's taco Tuesday, everyday. He enjoys reading; it's practically all he does, except bicycling. Send him an Email.

This is Adam's website. Like everything else, it's a work-in-progress. It includes his blog (which you can browse by the tags used to describe content), a list of books he has read, and a RSS feed if you want to subscribe. There is no tracking or analytics. The text is currently set in Inter. However, since he is a font nerd, as many bookish persons tend to be, this will likely change in the future.
