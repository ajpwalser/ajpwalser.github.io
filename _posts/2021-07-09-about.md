### Ahoy!
{:toc}

So, I've decided that “ahoy” is my [greeting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ahoy_(greeting)) of choice. Why? Because [pirates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edelweiss_Pirates). Probably not the ones you *argh!* thinking of. Anyways, I digress...

I'm Adam. This is my website and blog. Posts are organized by [categories](https://ajpwalser.github.io/categories) and [tags](https://ajpwalser.github.io/tags/). These also give you an idea of what I'm interested in. If you like my content, there's a [feed](https://ajpwalser.github.io/feed.xml) (often called RSS) available which allows you to subscribe and receive new content via a newsreader without divulging your email. If you are unfamiliar with the idea of feeds, check out [Aboutfeeds.com](https://aboutfeeds.com/) for a quick overview on how to get started.

### Third-Person Bio
{:toc}

Adam was born, raised, and lives with his partner and daughter in a windswept city near the Rocky Mountains on the Canadian Prairies. A war studies and library school graduate, he apparently can't tie his shoes properly and doesn't have a driver's license. And much to the chagrin of nearly every adult male he encounters, can't be bothered to watch the entirety of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Adam thinks it unwise to moon a werewolf or say “bite me” to a vampire. This is probably the only advice worth following on a bumper sticker. When not doing other things, he can likely be found poppin' a wheelie (on his BMX bike).
