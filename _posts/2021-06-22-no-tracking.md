My privacy policy is simple: I don't track you.<!--more-->

No user data is collected or retained. You won't find any web analytics, cookies, advertisements, or paywalls. This website is static, is served via HTTPS, and does not include third-party scripts or CDNs.

- no web analytics
- no cookies
- no third-party scripts

I respect your privacy. I encourage you to verify this using a browser extension like [UBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/) or [Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org/). You can also review the full source code at my [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ajpwalser/ajpwalser.github.io).
