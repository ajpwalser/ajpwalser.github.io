My privacy policy is simple: I don't track you.<!--more-->

No user data is collected or retained. There aren't any analytics, third-party scripts, or CDNs running on this website and no cookies are placed on your device. You also won't find any ads or paywalls.

I respect your privacy and encourage you to verify this using a browser extension like [UBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/) or [Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org/). You can also review the full source code at my [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ajpwalser/ajpwalser.github.io).
