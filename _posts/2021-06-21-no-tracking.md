My privacy is simple: I don't track you.<!--more-->

There's no analytics or cookies. Website user data is not collected or retained. 

There's no ads or paywalls.

I've also taken steps to ensure that others are not tracking you. This website is static. It's generated with Jekyll and hosted on GitHub Pages. Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure (HTTPS) is enforced providing a layer of encryption. There's no third-party scripts or content delivery networks (CDNs).

I respect your privacy. But don't take my word for it. I encourage you to verify this using a browser extension like [UBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/) or [Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org/). You can also inspect the Jekyll [source](https://github.com/ajpwalser/ajpwalser.github.io) code for this website.
