*My privacy policy is simple: I don't track you.*

No user data is collected or retained. You won't find any analytics, cookies, advertisements, or paywalls.<!--more-->

I've also taken steps to ensure that others are not tracking you whilst reading my blog. The website framework is static (meaning that is comprised of simple and flat HTML files with CSS and JavaScript). It's generated by [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) via [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). There's no unpatched or vulnerable CMS platform or database to exploit and no third-party scripts or CDNs. HTTPS is enforced providing a layer of encryption. 

I respect your privacy. I encourage you to verify this using a browser extension like [UBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/) or [Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org/). You can also review the full source code at my [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ajpwalser/ajpwalser.github.io)
