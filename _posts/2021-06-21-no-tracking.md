My privacy policy is simple: I don't track you.<!--more-->

No website user data is collected or retained.

You want find any analytics, cookies, ads, or paywalls

I've also taken steps to ensure that others are not tracking you. This website is static not dynamic. Flat HTML, CSS, and Javascript are generated by Jekyll and hosted on GitHub Pages. There are no third-party scripts or CDNs. HTTPS is enforced providing a layer of encryption.

I respect your privacy. I encourage you to verify this using a browser extension like [UBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/) or [Privacy Badger](https://privacybadger.org/). You can also inspect the Jekyll [source](https://github.com/ajpwalser/ajpwalser.github.io) code.
